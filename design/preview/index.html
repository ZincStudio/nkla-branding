03 · Components

The Kit

Pill-shaped buttons (33px radius) define the interaction language. Three variants cover all contexts — accent fill for primary CTAs, navy outline for secondary.

button-primary
Lime fill (#E2E43C), slate text (#54595F). Pill 33px. Highest emphasis. Hover → transparent.
button-secondary
Transparent, navy border (#223D72). For light backgrounds. Hover → navy fill + accent text.
button-primary (dark)
On dark bg: transparent with white border. Hover → lime fill.
icon-feature
💬
80px circle (scaled). Transparent bg, white icon. Used on dark navy sections.
